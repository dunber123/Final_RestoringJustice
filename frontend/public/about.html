About Restoring Justice

Restoring Justice is a 501(c)(3) non profit organization based in Houston, Texas. We provide holistic legal defense and social services
to clients in need of public legal defense. In addition to providing legal support, Restoring Justice provides clients with access
to social services such as nutrition, housing, clothing, transportation, immigration, disability, job training, substance abuse and others.
More than just social services, Restoring justice seeks to mitigate the effects of racial disparity and harm caused by arrests and incarcerations and to eliminate mass incarcerations.
Ultimately, Restoring Justice hopes to enact positive reform to our legal defense and criminal justice system to better enfranchise those in need of aid.
